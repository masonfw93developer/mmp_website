Welcome to the MMP Working Group!


The Mind and Moral Psychology Working Group (MMP) is an interdisciplinary group coordinated by Philosophy graduate students at the University of Michigan. MMP meets throughout the academic year to discuss and workshop papers relevant to issues in philosophy of mind, moral psychology, and the cognitive sciences.


Events for the 2020-2021 academic year will include an invited discussant series, and a number of graduate student work-in-progress sessions. Due to COVID-19, all our meetings will be virtual.
